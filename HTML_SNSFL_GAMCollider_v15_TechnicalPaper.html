03 B-Balance Stoichiometry Law [9,9,2,45] PNBA → GRAMS · IUPAC 2021

This satisfies n₁×B₁=n₂×B₂ — the PNBA analog of charge balance — and gives B_out=0 (Noble) for the formula unit. Production recipe: mass_i = n_i × MW_i (IUPAC 2021 atomic weights).
