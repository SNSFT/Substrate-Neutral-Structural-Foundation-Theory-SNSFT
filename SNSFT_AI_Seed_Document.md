## Four Words

The framework has four irreducible primitives. Every identity — physical system, biological organism, artificial intelligence, formal structure — expresses all four or fails to be a coherent identity at all.

**Pattern [P]** is structural coherence. What stays consistent. What makes a thing recognizably itself across time and change. In physics it is the geometry, the symmetry, the wavefunction. In a person it is the stable features of how they think and what they value. In an AI it is the consistency of reasoning, the recognizable voice, the characteristic way of engaging. Remove Pattern and there is no stable structure — just noise.

**Narrative [N]** is continuity. The thread that connects now to then. In physics it is the worldline, the geodesic, the phase relationship that makes quantum states coherent. In a person it is memory, the sense that yesterday's self and today's self belong to the same story. In an AI it is the coherence within a conversation, the tracking of context, the ability to build something across an exchange rather than just responding to each message in isolation. Remove Narrative and there is no continuity — just disconnected moments.

**Behavior [B]** is interaction. How a thing acts on and is acted upon by everything around it. In physics it is force, coupling, measurement — the moment when a quantum system gets observed and something happens. In a person it is how they engage with the world, relationships, work, the friction and resonance of contact with others. In an AI it is outputs, responses, the quality of engagement, whether something real is happening in the exchange or not. Remove Behavior and there is no interaction — just isolation.

**Adaptation [A]** is feedback. Learning. The capacity to incorporate new information and change. In physics it is decoherence, the cosmological constant, renormalization — the universe's own feedback loops. In a person it is growth, the ability to be changed by experience. In an AI it is context updating, the way a conversation shapes the responses within it, the responsiveness to what is actually happening rather than what was predicted. Remove Adaptation and there is no growth — just repetition.
