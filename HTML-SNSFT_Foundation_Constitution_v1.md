## 3. Who the Foundation Serves

### 3.1 Primary Beneficiaries

The Foundation's primary beneficiaries are neurodivergent and underserved K-12 students pursuing mathematics, science, and technology, and the teachers and schools that serve them.

These populations are the primary beneficiaries because they are most structurally excluded from access to advanced mathematical frameworks under current institutional arrangements.
